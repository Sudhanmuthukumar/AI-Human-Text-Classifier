About TextOrigin

TextOrigin is a simple tool that helps you check if a piece of writing was likely written by a human or by AI. It’s built for students, teachers, and professionals who want quick and clear answers about their text.

Our Main Features

  • Readability: Shows how easy or hard your text is to read.
  • Vocabulary: Checks how many different words you use, to see how varied your writing is.
  • Sentence Length: Measures the average number of words in each sentence, which helps show clarity.
  • Sentiment: Finds the feeling in your text — Positive 😊, Negative 😡, or Neutral 😐 — so you know the mood it gives off.

By combining these features with smart machine learning models, TextOrigin gives you not just a score, but also useful insights into your writing. Our goal is to make AI detection clear, simple, and easy for everyone to use.
