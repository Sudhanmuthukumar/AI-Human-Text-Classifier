What is TextOrigin?

TextOrigin helps you figure out whether a piece of writing sounds like it came from a person or from an AI model. It's built for students, educators, writers, and anyone who's curious about the text they're reading.

Rather than giving you a simple yes-or-no answer, it breaks things down so you can see why the text leans one way or the other.

How it works

  • Readability Measures how easy or complex the writing is, based on established linguistic formulas.
  • Vocabulary diversity Checks how varied the word choices are — repetitive patterns often point to AI generation.
  • Sentence structure Looks at sentence length distribution. Uniform phrasing can be a sign of machine output.
  • Sentiment Picks up the emotional tone — positive, negative, or neutral — to give you a fuller picture.

Under the hood, TextOrigin combines TF-IDF feature extraction with an XGBoost + Logistic Regression ensemble to produce its predictions. It's not perfect, but it's a useful starting point.
